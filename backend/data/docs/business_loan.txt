Business Loan Eligibility

To qualify for a business loan:

- Applicant must be at least 21 years old.
- Business must be registered.
- Annual turnover should exceed ₹10 Lakhs.
- GST registration and PAN card are mandatory.
- Loan amount ranges from ₹50,000 to ₹50 Lakhs.
- Interest rate starts from 10.5%.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What documents are required for a business loan?
A: The required documents include:
- PAN Card
- GST Registration
- Business Registration Certificate
- Bank Statements
- Aadhaar Card (if applicable)

Q: What is the minimum age?
A: The applicant must be at least 21 years old.

Q: What is the interest rate?
A: The interest rate starts from 10.5%.
